What We Do

Education
The foundation for our TAMID Chapterâ€™s success; learning basic business principles and applying them though a hands-on, interaction-based educational model.

Consulting
Our teams provide pro-bono remote consulting services to clients in Israel in areas such as product design, business development, research, competitive analysis, US market entry strategy and more.

Fund
Our fund-management team engages in a simulation stock competition against other TAMID chapters and also manages a real-money investment portfolio focused primarily on Israeli-linked securities.

Networking
Throughout the semester, TAMID brings in representatives from companies in a diverse group of fields to network with our members and explain what the company does along with what a job or internship may entail.

Fellowship
Arguably the pinnacle of the TAMID experience, select members participate in an immersive, all-expense-paid, summer internship in Tel Aviv at one of our partner companies in fields such as high-tech, venture capital and consulting.

Speaker Series
Part of our commitment to continuing education; providing students with opportunities to learn from and interact with prominent business professionals and academics who are experts in their fields.

Part of a Global TAMID Network
Today, TAMID consists of 46 chapters spanning the United States as well as Israel. By joining TAMID, you become a part of a network with thousands of hard-working young professionals

TAMID Pro
TAMID members continue to engage after graduation through mentorship programs, networking, social events, conferences and involvement with chapters.
